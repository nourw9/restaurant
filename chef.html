Born in Lebanon, Mabrouke knew straight away that the cooking life chose him,
which is why he started working at Bliss, a very popular local joint,
where over 20 years of culinary experience was injected into him. In 2017,
he decided to follow his dream of creating his own Middle Eastern comfort food restaurant, therefore joining forces with Ralph Shehfe and creating Lebanese
Diner, resulting in elevating the Lebanese food scene in Cairo forever. He now serves as the Head Executive Chef there.
